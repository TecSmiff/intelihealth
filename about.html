About Us

InteliHealth is an AI-powered healthcare concierge platform designed to simplify how people in Mauritius access medical care. We connect users to the right doctors, clinics, and services based on their needs, location, and preferences.

Our mission is to make healthcare smarter, faster, and more personal — ensuring that quality care is accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Values

  • Trust: We protect your privacy and data.
  • Innovation: We use AI to simplify healthcare access.
  • Empathy: We put people first in every decision.
  • Accessibility: Quality healthcare for all.
